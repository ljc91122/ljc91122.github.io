Juncheng Liu's Homepage

Juncheng(Jeffrey) Liu    

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at Institute of Computer Science and Technology, Peking University. I obtained my B.S. Degree in Software Engineering from School of Software, Dalian University of Technology in 2013. During Oct 2017 to Oct 2018 I visited visual computing group at Cardiff University supervised by professor Paul L. Rosin.

My research interests lie at computer graphics, computer vision and pattern recognition. Specifically, I work on algorithms of data-driven 3D shape analysis from large datasets. See my research experience here.
