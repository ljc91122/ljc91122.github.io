About Myself

I obtained my PhD degree in computer science from Peking University in 2019. During my PhD I visited Cardiff University as a visiting scholar working with Prof. Paul Rosin in the visual computing group. After finishing my PhD, I got a fixed-term contract of postdoctoral research fellowship from University of Otago. Currently I work in computer science department at University of Otago as a lecturer. My research interests lie at 3D graphics, machine learning and robotics. See my updated CV here.
