Juncheng Liu's Homepage

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at Institute of Computer Science and Technology, Peking University. I obtained my B.S. Degree in Software Engineering from School of Software, Dalian University of Technology in 2013. During Oct 2017 to Oct 2018 I visited visual computing group at Cardiff University.

My research interests lie at 3D shape processing including modeling, analysis and retrieval. See my updated CV here.
